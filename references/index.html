Ch 5 · HPRC pangenome 規模

SV calling · 長讀長 vs 短讀長

教學在 alignment 章節並列短讀長與長讀長 SV calling，需強調差距：Zook et al. 2020 Nat Biotechnol（GIAB SV benchmark on HG002）對短讀長 (Illumina) 與長讀長 (PacBio HiFi、ONT) SV caller 做正面比對，發現短讀長 SV caller 對 50 bp–1 kb 區間 recall 僅 ~30–50%，long-read caller (Sniffles2、pbsv、cuteSV) 可達 ~90%；對複雜 SV (inversions、translocations、tandem repeat expansion) 差距更大。實務建議：(1) 臨床或致病 SV 鑑定建議至少含 PacBio HiFi 或 ONT R10.4；(2) 短讀長僅適合 deletion > 1 kb 與已知 hotspot 區；(3) 結合 dipcall + Truvari 做嚴格 benchmark；(4) 報告 SV 時務必附 Tier 1 vs Tier 2 vs Tier 3 confidence 與 region BED。

The tutorial discusses short- and long-read SV calling side-by-side; the gap is substantial. Zook et al. 2020 Nat Biotechnol (GIAB SV benchmark on HG002) directly compared short-read (Illumina) and long-read (PacBio HiFi, ONT) SV callers: short-read callers achieved only ~30–50% recall for 50 bp–1 kb SVs, whereas long-read callers (Sniffles2, pbsv, cuteSV) reached ~90%; the gap widens for complex SVs (inversions, translocations, tandem-repeat expansions). Recommendations: (1) clinical or pathogenic SV calling should include at least PacBio HiFi or ONT R10.4; (2) short-read is acceptable only for deletions > 1 kb in known hotspot regions; (3) benchmark with dipcall + Truvari under stratified regions; (4) always report Tier 1/2/3 confidence and the region BED used.

HPRC pangenome · v1 → v2 版本差異

教學提到「pangenome 取代 GRCh38 作為參考」需註明版本：Liao et al. 2023 Nature 發布 HPRC v1 含 47 個 phased diploid assemblies（94 haplotypes）來自 47 個族群多樣的個體；後續 HPRC v2 (Wang et al. 2025 Nature) 擴增至約 130 個個體、加入 telomere-to-telomere 完整 chromosome Y、更新 minigraph-cactus 與 PGGB 兩種 graph 構建管線。實務注意：(1) 引用時務必標 release tag（v1.0、v1.1、v2.0）與 graph backbone（minigraph-cactus vs PGGB vs Minigraph）；(2) variant 座標仍需報告 linear projection 到 GRCh38 或 CHM13；(3) vg / PanGenie / GraphAligner 對 graph 版本敏感，pipeline 升級需重跑驗證。

The tutorial cites the HPRC pangenome as replacing GRCh38; version pinning matters. Liao et al. 2023 Nature released HPRC v1 with 47 phased diploid assemblies (94 haplotypes) from 47 ancestrally diverse individuals; HPRC v2 (Wang et al. 2025 Nature) expanded to ~130 individuals, added the telomere-to-telomere Y chromosome, and updated both minigraph-cactus and PGGB graph-construction pipelines. Practical notes: (1) always cite release tag (v1.0, v1.1, v2.0) and graph backbone (minigraph-cactus vs PGGB vs Minigraph); (2) variant coordinates still require linear projection to GRCh38 or CHM13; (3) vg / PanGenie / GraphAligner outputs are sensitive to graph version — pipeline upgrades require re-validation.

scLLM · zero-shot 過度宣傳

教學介紹 scGPT、Geneformer、scBERT 等 single-cell foundation model (scLLM) 時，常引用其作者 paper 強調 zero-shot cell-type annotation 與 perturbation prediction 能力。需提醒 Kedzierska et al. 2025 Nat Methods（"Assessing the limits of zero-shot foundation models in single-cell biology"）的獨立 benchmark：在 zero-shot cell-type annotation 任務中，scGPT 與 Geneformer 表現經常不如傳統 baseline（如 scANVI、Harmony + KNN、甚至 raw PCA + KNN），特別是在 batch-shift 與 rare cell type 上；scLLM 對 pretrain corpus 內出現的細胞型別 overfit。實務建議：(1) zero-shot 結果務必附帶傳統 baseline 比較；(2) fine-tuning 比 zero-shot 更穩定；(3) 報告時公開 pretrain corpus 與 test set 是否重疊；(4) gene set perturbation 預測仍需濕實驗驗證。

When the tutorial introduces single-cell foundation models (scLLMs) such as scGPT, Geneformer, and scBERT, the authors' papers often emphasize zero-shot cell-type annotation and perturbation prediction. Cite Kedzierska et al. 2025 Nat Methods ("Assessing the limits of zero-shot foundation models in single-cell biology") for an independent benchmark: in zero-shot cell-type annotation, scGPT and Geneformer often underperform classical baselines (scANVI, Harmony + KNN, even raw PCA + KNN), especially under batch shift and on rare cell types — they tend to overfit cell types in their pretraining corpus. Recommendations: (1) always report classical baselines alongside zero-shot results; (2) fine-tuning is more reliable than zero-shot; (3) disclose pretraining corpus and check for test-set overlap; (4) gene-set perturbation predictions still require wet-lab validation.

MERFISH · panel size 並非無限

教學在 spatial transcriptomics 章節介紹 MERFISH 時，需澄清 panel 規模上限：Chen et al. 2015 Science（MERFISH 原始論文）採 14-bit MHD4 code 設計、實測 ~140 genes；Moffitt et al. 2018 Science 擴展到 ~10,000 genes 但需多達 N=69 輪 hybridization、超過 24 小時 imaging 時間、且需 high-quality fixation 與 cleared tissue，實驗門檻顯著上升。實務考量：(1) 全轉錄體建議改用 Stereo-seq、Visium HD 或 Open-ST；(2) MERFISH 在 100–500 genes panel 仍是 spatial resolution 與 sensitivity 最佳組合；(3) panel 設計工具如 MERlin、MerfishTools 需先做 codebook simulation 評估錯誤率；(4) 引用時務必標明 panel size、imaging rounds、與 z-stack thickness。

When the tutorial discusses MERFISH in the spatial transcriptomics chapter, clarify the panel-size ceiling. Chen et al. 2015 Science (the original MERFISH paper) used a 14-bit MHD4 codebook with ~140 genes; Moffitt et al. 2018 Science scaled to ~10,000 genes but required up to N=69 hybridization rounds, >24 h imaging, high-quality fixation, and cleared tissue — the experimental bar rises sharply. Practical considerations: (1) for whole-transcriptome use Stereo-seq, Visium HD, or Open-ST; (2) MERFISH at 100–500 genes remains the best balance of spatial resolution and sensitivity; (3) panel-design tools (MERlin, MerfishTools) require codebook simulation to estimate error rates; (4) always cite panel size, hybridization rounds, and z-stack thickness.

Visium HD · 2 µm bin ≠ 單細胞解析

教學介紹 10x Visium HD 時常以「2 µm 解析度」為賣點，需澄清：Oliveira et al. 2025 Nat Methods（"Characterization of Visium HD Slide on FFPE samples"）系統比對顯示，2 µm bin 並非 single-cell resolution：(1) 每 bin 平均僅捕獲 ~1–3 UMI，需聚合至 8 µm 或 16 µm 才有統計力；(2) 單一細胞 (~10 µm 直徑) 通常跨越多個 bin，spillover 顯著；(3) 細胞邊界仍需配合 H&E image segmentation (如 Cellpose) 或 cell2location 等 deconvolution。實務建議：(1) 報告時務必註明 binning strategy (2 µm / 8 µm / 16 µm) 與 mean UMI per bin；(2) 真正的 single-cell spatial 建議用 Xenium、CosMx、Stereo-seq T 系列；(3) 對 cell-type mapping 結合 segmentation + deconvolution；(4) 避免「2 µm = single cell」的行銷誤導。

The tutorial sometimes markets 10x Visium HD as "2 µm resolution"; clarify: Oliveira et al. 2025 Nat Methods ("Characterization of Visium HD Slide on FFPE samples") systematically benchmarked Visium HD and showed 2 µm bins are not single-cell resolution: (1) each 2 µm bin captures only ~1–3 UMIs on average, requiring aggregation to 8 µm or 16 µm for statistical power; (2) a single cell (~10 µm diameter) typically spans multiple bins, causing significant spillover; (3) cell boundaries still require H&E image segmentation (e.g., Cellpose) or deconvolution (cell2location, RCTD). Recommendations: (1) always report binning strategy (2 / 8 / 16 µm) and mean UMI per bin; (2) for true single-cell spatial use Xenium, CosMx, or Stereo-seq T series; (3) for cell-type mapping combine segmentation + deconvolution; (4) avoid the "2 µm = single cell" marketing fallacy.

Container ≠ Reproducibility

教學常以「使用 Docker / Singularity / Apptainer container 即可達到 reproducibility」為結論，需引入 Grüning et al. 2018 Cell Systems（"Practical Computational Reproducibility in the Life Sciences"）與 BioContainers 立場：容器化只是必要而非充分條件。完整可重現性還需要：(1) 確切版本鎖定：image 標 sha256 digest 而非 :latest；(2) 引用資料庫快照：reference genome、annotation、Pfam、TCGA 版本 (含 release date)；(3) 隨機種子：alignment、variant calling、scRNA UMAP 都需固定 seed；(4) workflow manager：Snakemake / Nextflow 提供 DAG 與 provenance；(5) 原始資料保存：raw FASTQ 上傳 SRA/ENA 並提供 checksum；(6) 非確定性硬體：GPU floating-point 與多執行緒順序可能造成微小差異。Container 解決「環境」一層，其他五層仍需獨立處理。

The tutorial often concludes "Docker / Singularity / Apptainer containers give you reproducibility"; cite Grüning et al. 2018 Cell Systems ("Practical Computational Reproducibility in the Life Sciences") and the BioContainers position: containerization is necessary but not sufficient. Full reproducibility additionally requires: (1) exact version pinning: tag images with sha256 digest, not :latest; (2) database snapshots: reference genome, annotation, Pfam, TCGA versions with release dates; (3) random seeds: alignment, variant calling, scRNA UMAP all need fixed seeds; (4) workflow managers: Snakemake / Nextflow for DAGs and provenance; (5) raw data preservation: FASTQ uploaded to SRA/ENA with checksums; (6) non-deterministic hardware: GPU floating-point and thread ordering can introduce small numerical differences. Containers solve one layer (environment); the other five still require independent attention.
